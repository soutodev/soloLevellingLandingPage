Sung Jinwoo is the main protagonist of Solo Leveling. Originally an infamously weak E-Rank Hunter, he got the chance of a lifetime when he was selected as the Player of the System, giving him the unique ability to grow in strength without restraint. As a result, by taking advantage of the opportunities offered to him by the System, Jinwoo eventually rose to power as humanity's greatest hunter and the second Shadow Monarch.

Jinwoo is a confident individual with a strong sense of family and a thirst for power. As a result, he is very close to his sister, mother, and Jinho, and rarely wastes an opportunity to grow stronger.
